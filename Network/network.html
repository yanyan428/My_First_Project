TelecomNetwork I CIS 345

Course Overview

Welcome to Telecommunication Networks I -- CIS 345.
This course is an introductory course to computer networks. It covers the fundamentals of networking concepts, such as networking media, topology, switching, and management. It will also include an introduction to Open System Interconnection (OSI) layered organization and the functionality of each layer. Want to build, troubleshoot, and secure computer networks? This course will give you the skills to do just that! Network engineering is a hot field with plenty of job opportunities. Plus, understanding networks is a key skill for anyone in tech.
